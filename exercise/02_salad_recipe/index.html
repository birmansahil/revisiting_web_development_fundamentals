Caesar Salad

Classic Caesar Salad with crisp croutons and a light dressing is easy to make and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Two small heads of romaine lettuce
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Crisp croutons
  • Caesar salad dressing

Method:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine all of your ingredients
  2. Toss gently to coat the lettuce in caesar dressing
